Recipe Book Entry #8

This is a recipe for Matcha Swiss Rolls. These special rolls are light and fluffy, filled with rich whipped cream and wildberries. What contributes to this divine taste is the sourcing of these special ingredients.

    Use baking pans from your Mom's Home
    4 Eggs from The Easter Bunny
    Milk from The Cozy Cabin
    A basket of wild berries from The Wooded Peaks
    Heated over The Golden Flame found in Dragon's Cove
    A bundle of wheat from Windy Fields

Whisk up the egg whites until forming white, frothy peaks -- as stiff as that in The Wooded Peaks. Add sugar, one spoonful at a time to ensure thorough incorporation. Combine egg yolks with sugar, milk, oil, and extract, then sifting in the ground up wheat flour. Fold about 1/3 of your merengue with this mixture to form the batter and bake over The Golden Flame until the cake is puffed and lightly browned. Form rolls with the resulting merengue and berries, adding final flourishes to your liking.
